What we do

Search THOUSANDS of Plants

BotaniBuddy offers users a comprehensive platform to effortlessly explore and access information on thousands of plant species.

With personalized plant profiles, BotaniBuddy effectively tracks and provides tailored watering schedules, ensuring optimal care for each individual plant.
